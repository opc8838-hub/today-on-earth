Dear Mr. Lohmann,

A Viewer for Over 20 Years

A Responsibility I Feel I Must Take
———————————————————

Then the program stopped. It disappeared from the world. For people of my generation who grew up with it, there's a quiet sense of loss.

At the end of 2025, I asked myself seriously: could I actually create the program I've dreamed about for so long? A few months later, technology has caught up to the dream. I've built a working prototype.

It would mean a great deal to someone who grew up watching it, to finally know where it all began.


Thank You

Finally, thank you. For over 20 years I've listened to this music every day, and I've never had the chance to express gratitude to the people who made it. Whatever comes of this letter, Earth Live changed a boy's life in China. I just thought you should know that.

With gratitude,
